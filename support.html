常见问题

Q: 我的数据存储在哪里？

所有数据均存储在您的设备本地。如果您启用了 iCloud，数据会通过 Apple CloudKit 在您的设备间同步。我们无法访问您的任何数据。

Q: 如何将任务同步到系统日历？

前往「我的」标签页，开启「同步到系统日历」。日理会在系统日历中创建一个「日理 Daily」日历，您的任务会自动同步至该日历。

Q: 如何备份我的数据？

在「备忘」标签页中，您可以通过导出功能备份备忘数据。任务和日程数据会随 iPhone 备份（iTunes / iCloud 备份）一同保存。

Q: 如何删除所有数据？

卸载应用即可永久删除所有本地数据。如需删除 iCloud 中的同步数据，请前往 iOS 设置 > Apple ID > iCloud > 管理存储空间。

English

FAQ

Q: Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, data syncs across your devices via Apple CloudKit. We have no access to any of your data.

Q: How do I sync tasks to the system calendar?

Go to the "Settings" tab and enable "Sync to System Calendar". Daily will create a calendar named "Daily APP" in your system calendar, and your tasks will sync there automatically.

Q: How do I back up my data?

In the "Memo" tab, you can export your memo data using the built-in export feature. Tasks and events are included in your iPhone backup (iTunes / iCloud backup).

Q: How do I delete all my data?

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data. To remove synced data from iCloud, go to iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.
